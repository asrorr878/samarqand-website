About Samarkand

Samarkand is one of the oldest cities in Central Asia, located in modern-day Uzbekistan. It is known for its historical architecture, such as Registan Square and the Bibi-Khanym Mosque. The city was an important hub on the ancient Silk Road and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Top Places to Visit

  • Registan Square
  • Gur-e-Amir Mausoleum
  • Shah-i-Zinda
  • Bibi-Khanym Mosque
